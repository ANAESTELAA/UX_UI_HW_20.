Hi I am a UX/UI designer located in Salt Lake City,Utah. I enjoy the process of design.

PROTOTYPING

Prototyping is one of my favorite tools to use because it helps the team and stakeholder visualize the final product before it is launched.This gives us a better understanding of what we have to offer

USABILITY-TESTING

Conducting Usability-Testing is one of the best ways that I have found give you a real life perspective of your design. There are times when we feel our design is well executed until we have it tested and really see our flaws and this is way I feel strongly about doing it

STORYBOARDING

With storyboarding I have learned to visualize the users interactions with my product.Giving me an understanding of how the user will utilize it
